Getting Started

MindShelf helps you save, organize, and discover your next great read. Here's how to get the most out of the app:

  1. Save Books: Tap the "View Details" button on any book to see full information and ratings
  2. Organize: Set reading status (To Be Read, Currently Reading, Read) and priority levels
  3. Discover: Browse AI-powered personalized suggestions based on your reading preferences
  4. Share: Export and share your reading lists with friends

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I sync across devices?
Sign in with the same account on all devices. Sync happens automatically when you're connected to the internet.
Q: Can I import from Goodreads?
Yes! Go to Settings → Import → Select Goodreads and follow the steps to import your reading list.
Q: How do I share my reading list?
Tap the "Share List" button in My Books. Choose what to share (all books, to-read only, or high priority) and select how to send it (email, text, or copy link).
Q: Why isn't a book showing up in search?
We search multiple databases. If a book is very new or self-published, it may not be available yet. You can add it manually using the "Add Custom Book" feature.
Q: How does the AI recommendation system work?
MindShelf learns from the books you save, prioritize, and mark as read. The more you use it, the better the recommendations become. It tracks your favorite genres, subgenres, and book types.
Q: Can I use MindShelf offline?
Yes! Your saved books are available offline. You need internet to search for new books or sync across devices.
Q: What do the different priority levels mean?
Priority levels help you organize which books to read first. High Priority = your most anticipated reads, Medium Priority = books you're interested in, Low Priority = books you might read eventually.
Q: Where do the book ratings come from?
We aggregate ratings from three platforms: Goodreads, Amazon, and Apple Books, giving you a comprehensive view of what readers think before you commit to a book.

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we delete your data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.
